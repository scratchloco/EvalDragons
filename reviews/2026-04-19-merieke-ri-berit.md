## Commander axes

1. **Tap / steal** — Merieke steals creatures; untap combos break parity.  
2. **Artifact / combo** — Scepter line, untap engines, **Staff of Domination** / **Manifold Key** angles.  
3. **Control** — high counter density, **Teferi’s Protection**.  
4. **Reanimation / theft** — **Bribery**, **Rise of the Dark Realms**, **Reanimate**.

## Build value

- **High synergy density** for a known Merieke **combo-control** shell; premium interaction (**Mana Drain**, **Deadly Rollick**).  
- **Risk:** very **low traditional ramp/draw** for a deck that wants to assemble **multiple cheap artifacts** and protect the commander.

## Strategic summary

- Win by **locking** the table with **Merieke + untap** (or **Scepter/Reversal** mana) then **value** or **combo finish**.  
- **Weaknesses:** **Aggro** under early pressure, **Rule of Law** effects, **grave hate** for **Rise**, **Null Rod**-style for artifacts.

## Recommended play lines

1. **Early:** land + **Sol Ring** / Signet; hold **Swords** / **Plow** for key threats.  
2. **Mid:** land **Merieke** when you can **protect** (Greaves / boots / counters); start **clamp** only with fodder.  
3. **Combo:** assemble **Isochron + Reversal** with **mana rocks** producing enough; or **Freed from the Real** on Merieke with **blue mana** and tap/untap enabler. **Practice** the exact sequence in playtester.
