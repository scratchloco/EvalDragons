## The "Plan A" Audit (Strategic Summary)

* **The engine:** Build a token-creature board, leverage anthem/vigilance effects, and convert board presence into card flow through investigate and combat triggers.
* **The spice:** Warhammer-specific creatures create flavorful board states with wide attack synergies and artifact subthemes.
* **Tactical vulnerabilities:** Slow starts against tuned pods, vulnerability to repeated wipes if draw is not online, and occasional color-sequencing friction.

## Interaction & Protection Quality

* **Stack interaction density:** Moderate. You have some instant interaction but not a dedicated control package.
* **Board wipe asymmetry:** **Exterminatus**, **Hour of Reckoning**, **Martial Coup**, and **Fell the Mighty** can reset efficiently, especially when you rebuild faster with token engines.
* **Engine protection:** **And They Shall Know No Fear**, **Bastion Protector**, and **Vexilus Praetor** give useful commander/board resilience.

## Turns 1 to 4 Goldfish Simulation

Assumptions: on the play, no opposing interaction.

### Hand A (Ideal)

* **T1:** Land + one-mana setup or tapped fixer.
* **T2:** Two-mana rock.
* **T3:** Early creature/token setup.
* **T4:** Greyfax with immediate pressure or tap-down value line.

### Hand B (Grinder)

* **T1-T2:** Land sequencing and one rock.
* **T3:** Utility creature plus hold interaction.
* **T4:** Greyfax or value enchantment, then build wide.

### Hand C (Mulligan test)

* **Opening:** Three lands, no ramp, all 4+ MV spells.
* **Line:** Usually ship in stronger pods; this deck wants one cheap accelerator.

## Recommended Play Lines & Piloting

* **Opening hands:** Keep hands with at least one early ramp piece and one castable board-impact play before turn four.
* **Deployment timing:** Play Greyfax once you can either attack safely or generate immediate clue/value.
* **Closing the game:** Leverage wide board plus anthem/vigilance, then convert pressure with selective wipes that leave you advantaged.
